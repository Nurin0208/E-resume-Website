College Matrikulasi Pulau Pinang
( KMPP )

Details

    "College Matrikulasi Pulau Pinang," often abbreviated as Kolej Matrikulasi Pulau Pinang (KMPP),

  • Is a Pre-University institution in Pulau Pinang,
    Malaysia. It is one of several matrikulasi colleges established by the Malaysian Ministry of
    Education to prepare students for admission into Malaysian public universities.

  • These colleges offer one-year or two-year
    matriculation programs that are equivalent to the

    final year of high school (Form 6) and are

    recognized as a pathway to enter local universities.

  • KMPP specifically serves students in the northern region of Malaysia, providing them with a comprehensive academic curriculum to facilitate

    their transition to higher education.
